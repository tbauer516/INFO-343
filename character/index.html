Geralt of Rivia

Geralt of RIvia is a Witcher, which is more or less a monster hunter for hire. He resides in Kaer Morhen (a castle where witchers stay) when he is not traveling in search of gold. He has met many friends along his travels among them are many skilled craftsmen, sorceresses and people in power. He has killed many a monster and saved the world several times.
